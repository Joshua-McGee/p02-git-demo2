My Education

Very easy to work these to death. Learn when to stop. We're not trying to teach you a thing to copy. We're just here to teach you a technique, then let you loose into the world.

We don't want to set these clouds on fire. We spend so much of our life looking - but never seeing. See. We take the corner of the brush and let it play back-and-forth. Work that paint. Don't forget to tell these special people in your life just how special they are to you.

Who Am I

Ratings
