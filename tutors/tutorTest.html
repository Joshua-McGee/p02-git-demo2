My Education

I am a recent graduate of the Global Education Program at Simon Fraser University, a unique program that aims to integrate themes of peace and justice, human rights, environmental sustainability and international development into existing curricula. In my fourth and final year at school I had the opportunity to teach abroad in different parts of the world. I have taught in Kenya, France, South Africa, Australia, and Japan.

My education degree specializes in Mathamatics, English. I have written and published a number of studies in the journal of educational psychology. Through my travels and studies I have concluded that everyone on earth learns differenlty and the approach a teacher takes is instrumental in their success.

I am currently persuing a masters in educational psychology and tutoring as a part of my studies and as a source of income to help pay for my tuition.
